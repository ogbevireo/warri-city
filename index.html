Warri

Take a trip to explore the endengered Mangroove and Marine ecosystem that lies in the folds of the Niger Delta.

Top three activities to do on a trip to Warri

Falcorp Mangrove Park

Visit the Falcorp Mangrove Park. The park hosts a variety of animals from the mangroove swamps. It is a conservation center for endengered species. Enjoy the serene atmosphere.

The Warri Refinery

Take a tour of the Warri Refinery and Petrochemical Company. Get acquianted with the refining process of hydrocarbons. Enjoy the spectacle of the 'Christmas Tree' at night time.

Sumptuous Cuisines

Enjoy the unique cuisines that is rich in proteins. Eat freshly cooked traditional soups made from ingredients sourced directly from the environment. Both freshwater and saltwater animals.
